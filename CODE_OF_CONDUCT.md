# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Treenix to be a productive, professional, and welcoming project. Contributors and maintainers are expected to treat each other with respect across issues, pull requests, discussions, chats, events, and any other project space.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept correction when impact differs from intent.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on facts, tradeoffs, and code.
- Help maintain a clear path from problem to decision.
- Respect privacy and boundaries.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, intimidation, or sustained disruption.
- Insults, personal attacks, or derogatory language.
- Sexualized language or unwanted sexual attention.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Spam, trolling, or repeated off-topic behavior.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, block accounts, or take other action when behavior violates this Code of Conduct.

Reports can be sent to `support@treenix.io`. Please include relevant links, screenshots, context, and whether the report may be shared with other maintainers. Reports will be handled with care and confidentiality where possible.
